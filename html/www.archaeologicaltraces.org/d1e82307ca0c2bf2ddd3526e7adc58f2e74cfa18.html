Living Past: Online Magazine of Experimental Archaeology and Ethnography. ISSN 2037-7142. Living Past is an online magazine of Experimental Archaeology published by the A. T. P. G. Society. Its contents include simple videos, documentaries and other multimedia features the authors have to attach to their articles. It is an open access journal which means that all content is freely available without charge to the user or his/her institution.
