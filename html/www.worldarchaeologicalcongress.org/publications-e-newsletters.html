[First posted in AWOL 13 August 2009. Updated 20 November 2012]. World Archeological Congress e-Newsletters. The email newsletter of the World Archaeological Congress was established to facilitate communication between the WAC Executive and WAC members, and amongst WAC members. It is issued every two months. We invite members to submit material for inclusion in the newsletter.
