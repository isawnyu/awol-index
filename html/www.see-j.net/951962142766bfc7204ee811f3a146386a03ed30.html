[First posted in AWOL 9 December 2011, updated 3 January 2015]. Scandinavian Evangelical E-Journal (SEE-J). ISSN: 1904-2159. Scandinavian Evangelical E-Journal (SEE-J) is a peer-reviewed academic journal within the field of Biblical Studies in the evangelical tradition, published by the Copenhagen Lutheran School. Access to SEE-J is free, and it is possible to subscribe to a notification service on new editions to SEE-J. SEE-J continues SEE-JNTS and Nordisk Teologi (discontinued December 31, 2010). Articles published in the 2010 Volume of SEE-JNTS has been republished as volume 1 (2010) of SEE-J.
