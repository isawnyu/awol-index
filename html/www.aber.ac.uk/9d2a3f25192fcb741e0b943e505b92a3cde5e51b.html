ISSN: 0735-1348. <blockquote class="tr_bq"> Ancient TL is a journal devoted to Luminescence Dating, Electron Spin Resonance Dating and related techniques. It aims to publish papers dealing with experimental and theoretical results in this field, with a minimum of delay between submission and publication. Ancient TL has a reviewing system in which direct dialogue is encouraged between reviewers and authors. Ancient TL also publishes a current bibliography, thesis abstracts, letters and miscellaneous information, e. g. announcements for meetings. <ul><li><a href="http://www. aber. ac. uk/en/iges/research-groups/quaternary/luminescence-research-laboratory/ancient-tl/journal/06-12/">Latest Issue of Ancient TL</a></li><li><a href="http://www. aber. ac. uk/en/iges/research-groups/quaternary/luminescence-research-laboratory/ancient-tl/journal/">Previous Journal Issues</a></li></ul>The journal has been published since 1977. Since 2004 the journal has been available free online, as well as in hard copy for a small subscription.
