Journal of Conservation and Museum Studies Special Collections. The Journal of Conservation and Museum Studies (JCMS) has an overall focus on the care and exhibition of collection items. The scope thus includes conservation science, artefact studies, restoration, museum studies, environment studies, collection management and curation. Please contact the editors if you are not sure whether your research falls into these categories.
