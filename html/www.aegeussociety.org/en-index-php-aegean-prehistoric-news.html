Aegean Prehistoric News. The ‘Aegean Prehistoric News’ is an electronic mail that presents recent news about Aegean prehistoric archaeology. The e-mail is sent free of charge to friends (or members) of Aegeus. From 2012 onwards it is sent every three months, four times a year (in September, December, March and June). If you would like to receive the ‘Aegean Prehistoric News’, please type your e-mail address in the left hand column.
