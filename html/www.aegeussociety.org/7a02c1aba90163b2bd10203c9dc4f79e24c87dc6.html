The Newsletter on Aegean and Cypriot Prehistory

description
The Newsletter on Aegean and Cypriot Prehistory is published in digital form, ten times a year, on the 15th of every month (from 15 September to 15 June). The Newsletter is sent to the members of Aegeus as an e-mail attachment free of charges. It is also send, for one year, as a small token of gratitude, to friends who donate books to the Aegean Library. Aegean Book Reviews 12 September 2012 Review of Elizabeth Schofield, 2011.
