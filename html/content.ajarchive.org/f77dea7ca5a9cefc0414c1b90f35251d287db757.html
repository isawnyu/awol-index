Journal for Semitics - Tyjdskrif vir Semitistiek ISSN 1013-8471 The Southern Africa Society for Near Eastern Studies (SASNES) has its own journal, Journal for Semitics (JSem), first published in 1989. The journal is accredited by the South African Department of Education and is published twice a year. Occasionally a special third number may be published in a volume.
