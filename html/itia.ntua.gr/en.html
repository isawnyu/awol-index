description
ITIA. Languages: English | Ελληνικά Itia is a research team working on the fields of hydrology, hydrosystems management, hydroinformatics and hydroclimatic stochastics. It consists of 18 members; the team leader is Demetris Koutsoyiannis. The name "Itia" is not an acronym; it is Greek for willow tree. Itia is an open team and has collaborated with colleagues worldwide. Resources available at Itia include: A data base of ancient hydraulic works.
