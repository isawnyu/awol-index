Illuminations. HMML publishes Illuminations twice per year, a magazine-format newsletter with information about current projects, the people who keep HMML open, and the manuscripts we preserve. Summer 2009. Winter 2008-2009. Summer 2008. Fall 2007. Spring 2007. Summer-Fall 2006. Winter-Spring 2006. See the full List of Open Access Journals in Ancient Studies.
