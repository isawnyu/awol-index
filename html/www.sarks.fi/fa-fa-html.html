Fennoscandia archaeologica

description
Fennoscandia archaeologica. Published by The Archaeological Society of Finland. The aim of this publication is to encourage discussion within the discipline and to improve the standard of archaeological research by contacts on the interdisciplinary and international levels. Fennoscandia archaeologica has been published annually since 1984.
