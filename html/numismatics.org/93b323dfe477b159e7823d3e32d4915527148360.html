MANTIS: A Numismatic Technologies Integration Service The ANS collections database contains information on more than 600,000 objects in the Society’s collections. These include, coins, paper money, tokens, ‘primitive’ money, medals and decorations, from all parts of the world, and all periods in which such objects have been produced. Show search instructions. ANS policies on the acquisition and deacquisition of numismatic items are available online
