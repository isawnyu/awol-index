Human Origins

description
Human Origins is a British-based peer-reviewed interdisciplinary journal dedicated to human origins research and Palaeolithic archaeology. Funded by the Arts and Humanities Research Council, we offer a broad and interdisciplinary emphasis on Palaeolithic archaeology as well as primatology, osteology, evolutionary psychology, ethnography, palaeo-climatology, geology, anthropology and genetics (phylogeography). We are currently inviting submissions and welcome short interim reports from the. field (1000 words), medium-length discussions (3000 words), and longer research. contributions (6000 words). If you are interested in submitting to Human Origins please read our submission guidelines.
