Digital Library for International Research, Middle East Research Journals Project. <blockquote class="tr_bq">The Middle East Research Journals (MERJ) project, funded by a grant from the Institute of Museum and Library Services (2002-2006), provided digital access to five complete journals held at American Overseas Research Centers in the Middle East in the CAORC consortium.
