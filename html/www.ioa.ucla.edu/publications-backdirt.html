Backdirt: Annual Review of the Cotsen Institute of Archaeology at UCLA

description
Backdirt: Annual Review, which began as a newsletter in 1973, is the magazine of the Cotsen Institute of Archaeology that highlights the recent news, research and activities of the Cotsen Institute. The Cotsen Institute Press publishes the annual magazine each spring. 2014 Issue.
