The links below lead to files that replicate the current print edition of the CSBS/SCÉB Bulletin, with the exception of the Membership Directory, which has not been posted in order to protect members’ privacy. Beginning in 2009 the Bulletin is published online only, with the exception of hard copies that are placed in the National Library in Ottawa and the CSBS Archives at Queen's University. The individual files are configured as Adobe Acrobat. pdf (Portable Document Format). The Acrobat Reader is necessary to read them; if you do not have this software, please go to the Adobe website, where you can download a free copy for any of the major computing platforms.
