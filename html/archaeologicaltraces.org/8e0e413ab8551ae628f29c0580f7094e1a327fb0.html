ArchaeologicalTraces.org: DISSERTATIONS ARCHIVE SERIES ISSN: 2038-7083 This section hosts a series of B.A., M.A. and Ph.D. dissertations. The publishing of every dissertation has been authorized by the local Heritage officials and the Academic Staff who reviewed the work. The dissertations are published in original language with an abstract in English. Editors: Giandaniele Castangia
