Living Past: Online Magazine of Experimental Archaeology and Ethnography. ISSN 2037-7142. Living Past is an online magazine of Experimental Archaeology published by the A. T. P. G. Society. Its contents include simple videos, documentaries and other multimedia features the authors have to attach to their articles. It is an open access journal which means that all content is freely available without charge to the user or his/her institution. Users are allowed to read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, or link to the full texts of the articles in this journal without asking prior permission from the publisher or the author. This is in accordance with the BOAI definition of open access. All the contents of this magazine are peer-reviewed by our referees. All the contents of this magazine are peer-reviewed.
