[First posted in AWOL 22 July 2010. Updated 25 May 2013]. Traces In Time ejournal. ISSN 2038-7709. Traces in Time articles, book reviews and reports are focused on the Prehistory and Protohistory of Europe, Mediterranean Area, Near East and Africa, and on new methodologies in Archaeological Research. Contents regarding Open Access and Free Software in Archaeology will also be accepted. Any article, book review or report that does not fall within this range of topics will be considered and discussed within the Editorial Board.
