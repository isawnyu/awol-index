Karnak Temples Bibliography Online

description
CFEETK Online Bibliographical Project. The development of a new CFEETK archives database started in 2009 has required a new unified bibliographic management tool. To allow a wider diffusion of the researches on the temples of Karnak and offer the online library as complete as possible, a first version of this project, developed since 2010, is now available online.
