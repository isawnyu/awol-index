Chinese Archaeology: Institute of Archaeology, Chinese Academy of Social Sciences (IA CASS). Welcome! Chinese Archaeology website is hosted and maintained by the staff at the Institute of Archaeology, Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, the leading archaeological institute in China. You will find the latest archaeological discoveries, research works and other interesting data on Chinese archaeology here. Contact with any suggestions, or general comments about our website.
