OpenBibArt: History of Art Bibliography in Open Access. The enhancement site "OpenBibArt" offers open accessconsultation, to all the bibliographical references from the Répertoire d'Art et d'Archéologie and from the Bibliographie d'Histoire de l'Art produced by INIST-CNRS. This site was designed for art historians, on an international scale, researchers and on a broader basis professionals and art lovers.
