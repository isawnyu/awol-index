[First posted in AWOL 4 January 2013. Most recently 11 August 2014]. Open Context Projects and Collections. Each project represents a specific study or research effort conducted by a single investigator or a team of collaborating investigators. The data and documentation presented in a project can be freely used and referenced by the research community and public for re-analysis, comparison with other collections, visualization, or other applications provided data contributors are properly credited with citation. Open Context currently publishes 53 projects.
