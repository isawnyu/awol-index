Bulletin of the Council of University Classical Departments. <blockquote class="tr_bq">The Council of University Classical Departments is the professional forum for all teachers of classical Greek and Roman subjects in British Universities. It has four meetings annually: three meetings of the Standing Committee, and a plenary Council meeting in November, at which all UK Classics Departments (and remnants thereof) are represented (see the <a href="http://www. rhul. ac. uk/classics/cucd/constitution. html">constitution</a>, and "Contacts, Representatives, and Member Institutions" below). As well as serving as a voice for classical teachers in higher education on matters of academic and professional significance, CUCD collects annual statistics on students numbers in UK classical degree programmes, and publishes the <i><a href="http://www. rhul. ac. uk/classics/cucd/bulletin. html">CUCD Bulletin</a></i>, which is available on-line from volume 24 (1995), and continues to appear
