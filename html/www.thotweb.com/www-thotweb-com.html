Thotweb ÉgyptologieThotweb was created in 1997 by a group of fellow students in egyptology. Its aim is to help spreading egyptological information throughout the world and to stimulate international cooperation. At the time being, most of it is in french, but it will be eventually translated into english, except for the research articles, which will remain in their original languages.
