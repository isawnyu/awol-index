Chicago Demotic Dictionary (CDD) publishes sections on days, months, and numbers online The Chicago Demotic Dictionary announced the online publication of the sections on Days, Months, and Numbers of the Chicago Demotic Dictionary. Containing 425 pages of entries, these files represent the final stages of completion for the upcoming multi-volume print publication. Further information on the status of the project can be found in Professor Janet Johnson's recent article in Oriental Institute News & Notes 216 (Winter 2013).
