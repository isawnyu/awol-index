description
[First posted in AWOL 5 November 2009. Most recently updated 2 December 2014]. Oriental Institute Annual Report. The print versions of the Oriental Institute Annual Report are available for members as one of the privileges of membership. They are not for sale to the general public. They contain yearly summaries of the activities of the Institute’s faculty, staff, and research projects, as well as descriptions of special events and other Institute functions. 2013-2014 Annual Report.
