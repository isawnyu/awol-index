Studia academica Šumenensia. ISSN 2367-5446. The main purpose of this periodical is to allow various topics of the history and archaeology of the Balkans and South- Eastern Europe which are quite often highly controversial to be discussed by the broader scholarly of the region. This is why the SAŠ is published entirely in international languages - English, German, French, Italian, Russian and Spanish. In order to broaden the range of the discussion, an interdisciplinary approach will be employed and historians, archaeologists, classicists, epigraphists etc. will be invited and most welcomed. Volume 1: THE EMPIRE AND BARBARIANS IN SOUTH-EASTERN EUROPE IN LATE ANTIQUITY AND EARLY MIDDLE AGES, edited by Stoyan Vitlyanov and Ivo Topalilov. See the full.
