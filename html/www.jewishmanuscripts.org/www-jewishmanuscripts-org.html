The Friedberg Genizah Project (FGP)

description
is pleased to announce that, following an Agreement with the Oxford Bodleian Libraries, high-quality digital images produced by the Oxford digitization services for all Genizah manuscripts and fragments in these Libraries (about 25,000 images) will be displayed in the Friedberg Genizah website. FGP would like to thank the Bodleian Libraries and their Officers for their goodwill and their genuine spirit of cooperation in this endeavor.
