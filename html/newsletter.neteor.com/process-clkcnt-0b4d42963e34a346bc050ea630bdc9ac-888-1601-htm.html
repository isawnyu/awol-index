Arabic, Georgian and Syriac manuscripts from St. Catherine's Monastery Mount Sinai (Egypt) are now available on e-corpus. This work carried out by the Université Catholique de Louvain within the framework of project Manumed (digitization of microfilm); detailed records are being captured with titles and incipits etc. Arabic MSS : 202 manuscripts / 50 987 images Syriac MSS : 130 manuscripts / 27806 images Georgian MSS : 12 manuscripts / 3099 images
