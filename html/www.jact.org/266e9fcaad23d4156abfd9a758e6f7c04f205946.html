Ancient History Teachers’ Notes

description
Ancient History Teachers’ Notes. These teachers’ notes are intended to prepare teachers for the new OCR specification by covering the main aspects of each of the optional subjects. They contextualise the prescribed sources and identity which issues and sources are most relevant to the individual bullet points of the specification. They will also give relevant extra background information.
