Survey: Digital tools used in Byzantine epigraphy. This brief survey is being conducted by Joel Kalvesmaki (editor in Byzantine studies, Dumbarton Oaks), to understand the technology currently used by scholars who write about Byzantine inscriptions (seals, coins, weights, frescoes, etc. ). Responses will help those testing Dumbarton Oaks' new specialized inscription font, Athena Ruby.
