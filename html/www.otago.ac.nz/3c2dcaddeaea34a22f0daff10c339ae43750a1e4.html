Index (1992-). Scholia and Scholia Reviews (volumes 1-20) have published 862 contributions by 392 scholars and academics at 193 universities and other institutions in 36 countries. Scholia and Scholia Reviews completed its twenty-year joint series in 2011. The quality of the scanned pages in Volumes 1-13 (1992-2004) will be improved progressively in 2012-2013.
