Archaeoastronomy and Ancient Technologies. ISSN: 2310-2144. "Archaeoastronomy and Ancient Technologies" is international multidisciplinary scientific free online peer reviewed journal. It is dynamic platform for scientific publications and discussions of modern advances in archeology, archaeoastronomy and history of science. The journal increasingly used to target at professionals in the archaeology, astronomy and technologies, conducting research in the field of protoscience.
