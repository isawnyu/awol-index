Laetaberis: The Journal of the California Classical Association - Northern Section. On July 12th-13th, 1916, at the University of California Berkeley, several western associations coalesced into the Classical Association of the Pacific States. In 1969, the California Classical Association emerged as a separate body. It is a non-profit organization with a volunteer Board of Directors elected by Members.
