Patristics: Newsletter of the North American Patristics Society. ISSN: 0360-652X. <blockquote class="tr_bq">The North American Patristics Society is a scholarly organization dedicated to the study of the history, literature, and theology of ancient Christianity. Founded in 1970, the Society welcomes a diversity of disciplinary and methodological approaches and invites the participation of scholars at all stages of their careers; NAPS boasts a large and active graduate student membership. Among the Society’s major activities are the sponsorship of an annual conference in Chicago, Illinois, and the publication of <a href="http://patristics. org/journal/"><em>The Journal of Early Christian Studies</em></a>. <a href="http://patristics. org/membership/">Membership</a> in NAPS includes a subscription to the journal. <br/>Welcome to the Patristics archive.
