RAMBI The Index of Articles on Jewish StudiesRambi - List of Articles in Jewish Studies - founded by Dr. Issachar Joel in 1966. This is a selective list of articles in various fields of Jewish studies and the study of Eretz Israel and the State of Israel. Rambi based largely on the collections of the National Library. The articles listed Rambi Nlktim from thousands of journals - whether in print or electronically, and from collections of articles, in Hebrew letters, Latin or Cyrillic - and of reprints are sent to by the researchers. The database is updated every day. By the end of 2007 were recorded in about 300,000 records, out of about 20,000 different sources. Rambi Catalogue.
