Sylloge Nummorum Graecorum: Black Sea Coins in Danish Collections. The Royal Collection of Coins and Medals in Copenhagen and The Danish National Research Foundation's Center for Black Sea Studies are proud to present this data base of coins from the Black Sea area in Danish collections. The data base includes information and colour images of 828 coins from the three collections published in Sylloge Nummorum Graecorum (The Royal Collection of Coins and Medals in Copenhagen: 803 coins, Aarhus University, Denmark: 18 coins and The Fabricius Collection: 7 coins).
