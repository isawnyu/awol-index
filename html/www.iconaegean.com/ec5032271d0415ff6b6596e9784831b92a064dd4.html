IconAegean: Exploring Aegean Iconography. I feel privileged to have spent many years researching, writing about and sharing my knowledge of the seals, signet rings and sealings that are part of a golden age of art in the Bronze Age Aegean. You are invited to explore the subject through my lectures and publications as well as through my databases of the iconography of the seal images. Janice L Crowley.
