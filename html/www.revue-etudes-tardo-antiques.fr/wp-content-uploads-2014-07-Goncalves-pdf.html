- p. 137-156. Abstract: This article analyses the function of domestic leisure in the "being" and "seeming" of aristocracies of Late Antiquity in the West. In order to outline a new approach to the « ideology of otium », and its importance in belonging to an aristocratic network, this scheduling article seeks to connect the political rhetoric and use of the concept of otium with descriptions of concrete practices of leisure. I would consider domestic leisure as an issue of social and cultural changes of Late Antiquity. Contraintes chez les lettrés et les fonctionnaires impériaux 7. BERNADETTE CABOURET.
