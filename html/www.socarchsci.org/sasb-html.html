The Society for Archaeological Sciences Bulletin. ISSN: 0899-8922. The quarterly newsletter of the SAS contains short articles, laboratory profiles, SAS meeting summaries, recent publications and book reviews, and professional meeting announcements. The SAS Bulletin is automatic with SAS membership.
