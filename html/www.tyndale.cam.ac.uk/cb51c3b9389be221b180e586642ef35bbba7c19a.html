[First posted in AWOL 6 January 2009. Updated 25 January 2012] Tyndale Bulletin ISSN 0082-7118 The Tyndale Bulletin (formerly the Tyndale House Bulletin) which is an assessed journal, is published twice a year and is the journal of Tyndale House, Cambridge, and of the Tyndale Fellowship for Biblical and Theological Research. Offers of articles for publication are welcome. Contributions are expected to be compatible with the doctrinal basis of the House and Fellowship. Before submitting an article, please read our guidelines on Submission of Articles.
