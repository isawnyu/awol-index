Early Explorers in Egypt & Nubia. This blog is intended as both an instrument for researchers on early explorers in Egypt and Nubia, providing useful tools in the On-line Resources section (On-line books, Archives, Map Collections, Photo Collections, etc. ), and as a place to publish original documentation and research on the subject (i. e. List of travellers, Accounts, Letters, etc. ). Anyone who would like to contribute with suggestions or articles is warmly welcomed!.
