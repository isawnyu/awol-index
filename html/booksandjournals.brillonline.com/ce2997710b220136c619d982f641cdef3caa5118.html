description
Greek and Roman Musical Studies. ISSN: 2212-974X. E-ISSN: 2212-9758. Greek and Roman Musical Studies is a new journal that will publish research papers in the fields of ancient Greek and Roman music, including musical theory, musical archaeology and musical iconography in Classical antiquity, as well as on its reception in later times.
