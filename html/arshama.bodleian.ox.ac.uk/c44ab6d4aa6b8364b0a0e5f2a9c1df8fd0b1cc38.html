description
The Arshama Letters from the Bodleian Library (eds. C. J. Tuplin, J. Ma). This multi-parted, multi-authored work is a preliminary presentation of some of the findings and activities of the AHRC-funded Research Network which explopred the documents relating to the Persian satrap of Egypt, Arshama. This work should be considered as a forerunner of, or taster for, the forthcoming volume Arshama and Egypt: The World of an Achaemenid Prince (OUP). All parts can be freely downloaded for research and instruction purposes, and full citation of the editors and authors should be given. Vol. 1 Introduction (J. Ma, C. J. Tuplin, L. Allen) Vol. 2
