description
Hittite Epigraphic Findings In The Ancient Near East. <blockquote class="tr_bq">The present web-site has been developed with the purpose of creating a general of the epigraphic findings belonging to the Hittite Kingdom (1600 – 1150 BC). The findings listed here concern, and written either in Akkadic or Hittite. <br/>. Data come from more than <em>70 archaeological sites</em> spread over a territory extending from the Western Anatolian coast to the Tigris valley and from the Northern Anatolian coast to the Syro-Lebanese border. <br/><br/>The web-site and its contents have been created and are currently curated by Dr. Dario Fossati.
