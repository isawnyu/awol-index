O. ) group of websites Egyptological was founded by Andrea Byrnes and Kate Phizackerley in 2011. If you have an interest in Ancient Egypt we hope that you will find articles, exhibition, book and DVD reviews, and much more value-added content to keep you entertained and informed. <br/>Egyptological provides an environment for writers and researchers of all levels of experience.
