Diane Scherzler on Syrian Cultural Heritage on Storify. Being an archaeologist by education, Diane Scherzler works as a journalist and project manager at Südwestrundfunk (SWR), a major German Public Broadcasting Company. She teaches science communication and writing skills at several universities and research institutions. Diane serves as vice chairperson of Deutsche Gesellschaft für Ur- und Frühgeschichte (German Society for Pre- and Protohistory) and is responsible for the society's monthly newsletter. Diane is founder and head of the Euroscience workgroup "Science Communication".
