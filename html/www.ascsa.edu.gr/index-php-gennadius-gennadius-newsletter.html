Gennadeion News. Newsletter of the Gennadius Library. Gennadeion News is published by the ASCSA Development Office and sent free of charge to friends of the Gennadius Library approximately twice a year. Past issues are available to download as PDFs. The current issue of of Gennadeion News is the Spring 2012 Edition. Download the current issue. Archived back issues: Fall 2011. Spring 2011. Fall 2010.
