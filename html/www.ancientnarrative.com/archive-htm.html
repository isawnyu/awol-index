description
Ancient Narrative Supplementum. ISSN: 1568-3540. Ancient Narrative publishes regular volumes and supplements, which appear both in print and online. AN is published continuously, not in separate issues, but after the end of the year a volume containing all articles of the past year will appear simultaneously in print and on this website.
