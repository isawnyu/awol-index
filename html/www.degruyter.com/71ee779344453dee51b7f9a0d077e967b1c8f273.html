Have you taken the. AWOL User Survey ? Materiale Textkulturen [Material Text Cultures]. Ed. by Lieb, Ludger. eISSN: 2198-6940 The series Material Text Cultures is the publication organ of the identically named Collaborative Research Center 993 at Heidelberg University. The series publishes anthologies and monographs dedicated to the  Collaborative Research Center’s main focus of research - that is, the materiality and presence of the written in non-typographic societies.
