Virtual Exhibitions at The (Hellenic) National Archive of Monuments

description
The (Hellenic) National Archive of Monuments Virtual Exhibitions This section contains virtual exhibitions of the monuments that have been digitised. The themes are polysemous, while the criteria for the organization of the material include temporal and spatial aspects, and material as well as symbolic/interpretative features. The virtual exhibitions will be renewed on a regular basis.
