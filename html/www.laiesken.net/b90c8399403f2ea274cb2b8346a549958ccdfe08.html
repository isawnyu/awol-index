[First posted in AWOL 19 January 2010. Updated 3 January 2012]. Arqueología Iberoamericana. ISSN: 1989-4104. ARQUEOLOGIA IBEROAMERICANA is a peer-reviewed international scientific journal, open access, devoted to the archaeological study of prehistoric, protohistoric, ancient and historical societies of Latin America, United States, the Iberian Peninsula, the Philippines, and other Iberian countries, whose primary objective is to serve the research community with the highest quality and speed and non-profit one.
