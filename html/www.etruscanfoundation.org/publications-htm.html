[First posted in AWOL 9 December 2011. Updated 23 April 2013]. Etruscan Studies is the leading scholarly journal on Etruscology and related disciplines in the English language. It details activities in all areas of research and study related to the Etruscan civilization with articles contributed by scholars around the world, as well as reviews of meetings and publications of interest to the professional community. The editor and editorial board are drawn from members of the Etruscan Foundation's Advisory Board. The following volumes are accessible at the.
