The Johns Hopkins Archaeological Museum. The mission of the museum is to inspire and facilitate the study of the ancient world by providing faculty and students access to a remarkable collection of archaeological artifacts. The museum is dedicated to: The preservation of the collection Museum quality display and exhibitions The accessibility of the collection for teaching, study and research by faculty and students Interdisciplinary and collaborative research.
