Athenian Agora Preliminary Reports. [Agora Report] 2013 Excavations: Preliminary Report on the 2013 Excavation Season. John Camp. This report is based on one prepared for the student volunteers for them to take away at the end of the season. It is therefore to be regarded as very preliminary, and subject to considerable alteration. [Agora Report] 2012 Excavations: Preliminary Report on the 2012 Excavation Season. John Camp. Excavations were carried out in the Athenian Agora from June 11 to August 3, 2012 with a team of some 60 student volunteers, representing 30 universities and eight countries. This is a very preliminary. 11 Jun-3 Aug 2012 [Agora Report] 2011 Excavations: Preliminary Report on the 2011 Excavation Season. John Camp. Excavations in the Athenian Agora were conducted by the American School of Classical Studies at Athens from June 13th to August 5th, 2011. The work was carried out in three sections, two of them overlying. 13 Jun-5 Aug 2011 [Agora Report] 2010 Excavations: Preliminary Report on the 2010 Excavation Season John Camp. 15 Jun-6 Aug 2010 [Agora Report] 2010 Excavations Trophy: A Sculpted Trophy from the Athenian Agora: Preliminary Report.
