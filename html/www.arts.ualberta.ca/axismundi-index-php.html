Axis Mundi is an online journal edited and maintained by Religious Studies graduate students at the University of Alberta. We accept contributions from students in any year of study-undergraduate and graduate-in universities and colleges across North America. We encourage submissions pertaining to any aspect of the academic study of religion. Axis Mundi is sponsored by the University of Alberta Religious Studies Program and affiliated with the UofA Religious Studies Graduate Students Society (RSGSS). m of Star Wars.
