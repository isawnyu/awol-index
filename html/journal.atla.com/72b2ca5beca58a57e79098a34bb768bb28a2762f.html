ISSN 1937-8904. <blockquote class="tr_bq"><div id="header"><div id="headerTitle"><h1> </h1></div></div>The purposes of Theological Librarianship are: to foster the professional development of theological librarians and to contribute to and enrich the profession of theological librarianship. <br/>. TL publishes essays, columns, critical reviews, bibliographic essays, and peer-reviewed articles on all aspects of professional librarianship, within the context of a religious/theological library collection encompassing interactions with faculty and administrators engaged in religious/theological education.
