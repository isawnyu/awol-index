Updated 27 December 2012] Teiresias: A Review and Bibliography of Boiotian Studies ISSN 1206-5730 Teiresias (ISSN 1206-5730) is a review and continuing bibliography of Boiotian studies. It has been published since 1971, and since 1991 has been available electronically. It is edited by Albert Schachter, Emeritus Professor of History.
