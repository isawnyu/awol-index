The contents consist mainly of reports on salvage archaeology projects conducted by Y. G. Archaeology under NGSBA oversight. The journal is peer reviewed, edited by David Ilan, the director of the NGSBA, and is overseen by a board of editors. It will appear more or less annually—depending on the quantity of material available for publication—in print and digital form.
