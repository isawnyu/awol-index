Archaeologia Aeliana. The Society of Antiquaries of Newcastle upon Tyne promotes the preservation, study and enjoyment of historical and archaeological heritage in general, and of the North East of England in particular. We have over 750 members, and always welcome new ones. Most of the volumes of Series 1 and 2 of (1822-1904 and therefore out of copyright) have been digitised and are available on the Internet via not-for-profit site www. archive. org. These are listed below. Click on the volume number to see the volume concerned. Some volumes of the Third Series are also available in this way; these are marked on the Contents page.
