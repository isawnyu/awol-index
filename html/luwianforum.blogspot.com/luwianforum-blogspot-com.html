Luwian Forum. Welcome to the Luwian forum. This is meant as a meeting place, to exchange news, ideas, share publication details etc. Add anything you like via the comment section or send me an email if you'd like to write a new post. We'll begin with a list of Bronze Age Inscriptions and their publication details. Each text will form a single post to facilitate adding comments to it. They'll appear in no particular order. Please help fill the gaps!.
