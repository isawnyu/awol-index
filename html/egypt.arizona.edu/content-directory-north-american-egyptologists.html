Directory of North American Egyptologists. Since 1988 the UAEE has published the Directory of North American Egyptologists which provides the names and contact data for professional Egyptologists and current doctoral students in North America, as well as listing current and recent doctoral dissertations in Egyptology. From 1996 to 2014 the DNAE was co-published with the Oriental Institute of the University of Chicago. Navigate to: INDIVIDUALS. INSTITUTIONS. Listings Outside of North America.
