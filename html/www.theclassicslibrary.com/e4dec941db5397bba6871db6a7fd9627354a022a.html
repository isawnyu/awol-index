Classics Library Journal CL Journal Submissions Invited on the following theme, and always welcome on any topic. Theme for Forthcoming Issue (January 2014) CL members are invited to write articles to share and publish here in a digital journal format for all other members, posterity, and to share with the wider Classics community. Articles will appear here ‘in real time’, published online as soon as possible after they are submitted, and will also be collated and published as a freely available pdf/ebook, twice yearly (January and July).
