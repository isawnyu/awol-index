Aestimatio: Critical Reviews in the History of Science

description
[First posted in AWOL 15 October 2009. Most recently updated 30 July 2014]. Aestimatio: Critical Reviews in the History of Science. Aestimatio provides critical, timely assessments of books published in the history of what was called science from antiquity up to the early modern period in cultures ranging from Spain to India, and from Africa to northern Europe. The aim is to allow reviewers the opportunity to engage critically both the results of research in the history of science and how these results are obtained.
