description
The SAA Archaeological Record. Launched in January 2001, The SAA Archaeological Record is issued five times a year. It is a four-color magazine encompassing SAA business, commentary, news, regular columns, software reviews, job listings, opinions, and articles. The SAA Archaeological Record replaced The SAA Bulletin (view back issues). 2013 - Volume 13.
