Labyrinth: An on-line classical studies journal published by the Department of Classical Studies at the University of Waterloo. ISSN: 0318-8450. Welcome to the Labyrinth, an on-line classical studies journal published by the Department of Classical Studies at the University of Waterloo! This on-line journal is published three times annually and is intended for the use of high school teachers and their students. Here you will find past and present issues as well as information about our department. This website is currently under construction - new content will be added on a regular basis, so check back regularly! October 2010.
