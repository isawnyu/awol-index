Auteur: Mark de Brito. Résumé: "The thrust of this symposium is to interpret Egyptian civilization in the context of present-day and other African cultures. An impressive array of contributors, European and African, pursue the theme accross several disciplines: theory (epistemology), language, comparative religion, anthropology, history, prehistory, and archaeology. ". Cet article est paru dans les Cahiers Caribéens d'Egyptologie n°3/4.
