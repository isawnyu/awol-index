description
(30 June-2 July 2006), the first supplement to Herculaneum Archaeology, includes contributions by Francesca Longo Auricchio (Università degli Studi di Napoli Federico II), "The International Centre for the Study of the Herculaneum Papyri (CISPE)", Antonio De Simone (Università degli Studi Suor Orsola Benincasa - Napoli), "Herculaneum and the Villa of the Papyri: Studies, Excavation, and Prospects", and Carlo Knight, "Sir William Hamilton’s Neapolitan houses", on an early enthusiast for the eighteenth-century discoveries at Herculaneum. Earlier issues of Herculaneum Archaeology remain available:.
