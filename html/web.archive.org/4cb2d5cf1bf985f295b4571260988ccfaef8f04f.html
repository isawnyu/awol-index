[First posted in AWOL 30 November 2009. Updated 22 September 2013]. This newsletter is now restricted to member of the society. The first twelve issues remain openly accessible at the Internet Archive]. Herculaneum Archaeology. (The Friends of Herculaneum Society). Papers from the First Naples Conference (30 June-2 July 2006), the first supplement to Herculaneum Archaeology, includes contributions by Francesca Longo Auricchio (Università degli Studi di Napoli Federico II), “The International Centre for the Study of the Herculaneum Papyri (CISPE)”, Antonio De Simone (Università degli Studi Suor Orsola Benincasa - Napoli), “Herculaneum and the Villa of the Papyri: Studies, Excavation, and Prospects”, and Carlo Knight, “Sir William Hamilton’s Neapolitan houses”, on an early enthusiast for the eighteenth-century discoveries at Herculaneum. Earlier issues of Herculaneum Archaeology remain available:.
