Notes on KIDa The CDLN has recently changed its publication practices. New submissions will appear in preprint status four times a year (January 1, April 1, July 1, and October 1; notices of new submissions will be made to this list). During the preprint period, authors will be able to make small, non-substantive changes (e.g. typographical errors) to their submissions. After two weeks, these submissions are then archived.
