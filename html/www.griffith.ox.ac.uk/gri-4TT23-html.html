Tracings made in various Theban tombs by Norman and Nina de Garis Davies, and others. Some 1,000 sheets of tracings made in various Theban tombs by. Norman and Nina de Garis Davies sometime between 1920 and. 1940, now in the Archive of the Griffith Institute, will be made. available on the Institute's website.
