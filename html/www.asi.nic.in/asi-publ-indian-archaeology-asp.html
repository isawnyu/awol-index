[First posted in AWOL 31 August 2009. Updated 14 March 2012]. Indian Archaeology - A Review. ‘Ancient India’ the Bulletin of the Archaeological Survey of India was started in 1946, which contained general and research articles on different aspects of archaeology in India and adjacent countries. The first issue of ‘Indian Archaeology 1953-54 - A Review’ was published in 1954, which provides information about all important archaeological activities carried out in the country each year. The latest one published is the issue for the year 1999-2000 and two issues for the year 2000-2001 and 2001-2002 are in press. The remaining issues are in the various stages of preparation.
