description
[First posted in AWOL 31 August 2009. Updated 14 March 2012]. Indian Archaeology - A Review. <blockquote>‘Ancient India’ the Bulletin of the <a href="http://www. asi. nic. in/">Archaeological Survey of India</a> was started in 1946, which contained general and research articles on different aspects of archaeology in India and adjacent countries. <br/>. The first issue of ‘Indian Archaeology 1953-54 - A Review’ was published in 1954, which provides information about all important archaeological activities carried out in the country each year. The latest one published is the issue for the year 1999-2000 and two issues for the year 2000-2001 and 2001-2002 are in press. The remaining issues are in the various stages of preparation. <a href="http://www. asi. nic. in/asi_publications. asp">[more]</a> </blockquote> Archaeological Survey of India <blockquote>‘Ancient India’ the Bulletin of the <a href="http://www. asi. nic. in/">Archaeological Survey of India</a> was started in 1946, which contained general and research articles on different aspects of archaeology in India and adjacent countries. <br/>.
