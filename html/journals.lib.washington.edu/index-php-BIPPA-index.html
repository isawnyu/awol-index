[First posted in AWOL 15 June 2009. Updated 20 June 2012]. Bulletin of the Indo-Pacific Prehistory Association. The Bulletin of the Indo-Pacific Prehistory Association is an international, peer-reviewed, open access online journal. It's purpose is to disseminate rapid communications and field reports on the archaeology of East Asia, Southeast Asia and the Pacific.
