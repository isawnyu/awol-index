[First posted in AWL 2 June 2011. Updated 6 November 2014]. Orientalia Suecana. ISSN 2156-2253. Orientalia Suecana is an international journal of Indological, Iranian, Semitic, Sinological and Turkic studies founded in 1952 and published anually or biannualy. Further information about the journal is available here, including information about earlier volumes and an electronic order form.
