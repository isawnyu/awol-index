Bibliography of Sumerian Literature

description
Bibliography of Sumerian Literature, derived from the Oxford project "Electronic Text Corpus of Sumerian Literature". When the CDLI inherited the Mesopotamian Year-Names project of Peter Damerow and Marcel Sigrist, that in the meantime has been expanded, and for many Lagash II and Ur III year names corrected by Richard Firth, we decided to incorporate this work into a broader presentation of the chronology of Mesopotamia.
