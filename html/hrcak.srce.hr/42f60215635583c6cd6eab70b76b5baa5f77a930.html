Starohrvatska prosvjeta

description
Starohrvatska prosvjeta ISSN: 0351-4536 Starohrvatska prosvjeta, which marked its 100 years publication in 1995, is the first scientific journal not only in Croatia, but also in the entire Slavic South, dedicated exclusively to topics from national archaeology. The programme, which was drawn up for the first issue, has not been significantly altered. Starohrvatska prosvjeta, having a variety of different topics that deal with all important scientific problems, informs about the latest research works conducted in that field and publishes site-reports, but also dedicates entire special volumes to significant sites and findings.
