Tenth International Congress of Coptic Studies, Rome, 17-22 September 2012. Plenary sessions, during which invited specialists will give reports on recent developments in the major domains of Coptic studies, according to the following (alphabetical) list: Alessandro Bausi, Ethiopic literary production related to the Egyptian culture Heike Behlmer, Coptic Literature (2008-2012) Heinzgerd Brakmann, Coptic Liturgy (2004-2012) Andrew Crislip, Shenoutean studies Alain Delattre, Documentary papyrology (2008-2012) James E. Goehring, Egyptian Monasticism (2008-2012) Karel C. Innemée, Coptic Archaeology (2008-2012) Gertrud J. M. van Loon, Coptic Art (2008-2012) Tonio Sebastian Richter, Coptic Linguistics (2008-2012) Sofía Torallas Tovar, Coptic Codicology and Palaeography (2004-2012) Gregor Wurst, Gnosticism and Manichaeism (2008-2012).
