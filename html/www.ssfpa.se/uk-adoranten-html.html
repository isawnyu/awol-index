description
[First posted in AWOL 27 July 2009. Updated 4 November 2012]. Adoraten. ISSN 0349-8808. ADORANTEN, an international magazine mainly concerned with rock carvings - recent research, interpretations, the history of religion, descriptions of rock carving localities around the world and literary reviews - but it also containes articles on any prehistoric subject which can be related to the field of rock carvings. The articles are in various languages, mainly English, German, Norwegian, Swedish and Danish.
