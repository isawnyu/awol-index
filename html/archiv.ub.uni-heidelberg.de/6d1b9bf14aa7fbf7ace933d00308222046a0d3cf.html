Journal of the International Association of Buddhist Studies. ISSN 0-193-600XX. The Journal of the International Association of Buddhist Studies (ISSN 0-193-600XX) is the organ of the International Association of Buddhist Studies. The JIABS welcomes scholarly contributions in all areas of Buddhist Studies. A double-blind peer-review process is used to ensure the high academic quality of all contributions. This website offers full access to the JIABS. Current issues will be available online 60 months after their appearance in print. We apologize that, for technical reasons, it is currently not possible to make available the most recent issues to IABS members through this website.
