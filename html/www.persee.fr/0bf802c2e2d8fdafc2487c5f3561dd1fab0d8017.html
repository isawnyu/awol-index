Revue Théologique de Louvain. eISSN - 1783-8401. "Founded in 1970, the Revue Théologique de Louvain is open to all areas of theology. The Journal has been under the editorial direction of Professors G. Thils, A. Houssiau, J. Ponthot, P. -M. Bogaert, A. Haquin, J. Scheuer and, since 2009, Professor C. Focant. The Journal, which publishes approximately 650 pages per year, features Articles and Short Notes, Book Reviews and Bibliographic Notices, Chronicles, and the annual International index of doctoral dissertations in Theology and Canon Law.
