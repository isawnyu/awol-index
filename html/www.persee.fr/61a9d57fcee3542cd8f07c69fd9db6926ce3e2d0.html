description
Cahiers de Linguistique - Asie Orientale. eISSN - 1960-6028. "The Cahiers de Linguistique – Asie Orientale (CLAO) is an international journal for the scientific study of languages in the Far East. Based in Paris at the Centre de Recherches Linguistiques sur l’Asie Orientale (CRLAO, EHESS-CNRS), it appears twice a year and is internationally refereed as an A - class journal. ".
