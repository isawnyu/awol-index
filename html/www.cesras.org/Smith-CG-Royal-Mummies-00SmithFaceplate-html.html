description
G. E. : The Royal Mummies, (Catalogue Général des Antiquités Égyptiennes du Musée du. Caire, 1912: CG numbers 61051-61100); complete text with modern annotations and digitally improved plates; Linked navigation to parallel material on this site. Smith gives a detailed forensic pathological study of the. royal mummies. This material is well illustrated and still valid today. They were not yellow (21a Dynasty painted anthropoid coffins from the "Royal Cache" TT320). Photographs by Sergej V. Ivanov to lecture by Edward R. Loring (Proceedings of the International. conference Achievements and Problems of Modern Egyptology held by the Centre for Egyptological Studies of the Russian Academy of Sciences, Sept. 29-Oct. 2, 2009 in Moscow.
