ASMOSIA Newsletters. ASMOSIA's [The Association for the Study of Marble and Other Stones In Antiquity] mission is to promote the exchange of knowledge among its members in all fields related to the study of marble and other stones of art historical or archaeological interest. This exchange is accomplished through periodic Newsletters to members, biannual meetings of the membership, and publication of the meeting proceedings.
