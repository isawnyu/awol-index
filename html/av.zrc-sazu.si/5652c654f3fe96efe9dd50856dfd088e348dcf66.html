description
Bibliographia archaeologica Slovenica selecta (BASS) <blockquote class="tr_bq"> Our intention is to present a continuous review of archaeological literature by Slovenian authors and a selection of foreign publications dealing with the Slovenian territory and materia, divided into four parts. The first part comprises publications by Slovenian authors abroad, the second part is composed of publications by foreign authors, relevant for Slovenian territory and material, while the third part includes works of Slovenian authors published in Slovenia, excepting the basic archaeological publications (Arheološki vestnik, Arheo, Varstvo spomenikov, Documenta Praehistorica, Annales) till the 60th issue. The last, fourth part includes short reviews of recently published archaeological monographs in Slovenia. <div class="poudarki">BASS for:</div><ul><li><a href="http://av. zrc-sazu. si/pdf/BASS/BASS_2000_2003_AV_55. pdf">2000–2003</a></li><li><a href="http://av. zrc-sazu. si/pdf/BASS/BASS_2004_AV_56. pdf">2004</a></li><li><a href="http://av. zrc-sazu. si/pdf/BASS/BASS_2005_AV_57. pdf">2005</a></li><li><a href="http://av. zrc-sazu. si/pdf/BASS/BASS_2006_AV_58. pdf">2006</a></li><li><a href="http://av. zrc-sazu. si/pdf/BASS/BASS_2007_AV_59. pdf">2007</a></li><li><a href="http://av. zrc-sazu. si/pdf/BASS/BASS_2008_AV_60. pdf">2008</a></li><li><a href="http://av. zrc-sazu. si/pdf/BASS/BASS_2009_AV_61. pdf">2009</a></li><li><a href="http://av. zrc-sazu. si/pdf/BASS/BASS_2010_AV_62. pdf">2010</a></li></ul></blockquote> BASS for: 2000–2003 2004 2005 2006 2007 2008 2009 2010.
