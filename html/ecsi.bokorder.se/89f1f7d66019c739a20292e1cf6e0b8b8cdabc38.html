[First posted in AWOL 14 September 2012, updated 24 February 2014]. Opuscula: Annual of the Swedish Institutes at Athens and Rome. Opuscula is published yearly by the Swedish Institutes at Athens and Rome. First issued in 2008 (no. 1), Opuscula replaces the annuals Opuscula Atheniensia and Opuscula Romana published by the Swedish Institute at Athens and the Swedish Institute in Rome respectively.
