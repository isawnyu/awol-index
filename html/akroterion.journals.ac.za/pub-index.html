Akroterion: Journal for the Classics in South Africa. Tydskrif vir die Klassieke in Suid-Afrika. ISSN 2079-2883 (online)ISSN 0303-1896 (print). Akroterion welcomes scholarly contributions on all aspects of Greek and Roman civilization. Preference is given to articles that will also appeal to the non-specialist. We particularly encourage submission of articles dealing with the influence and reception of the Classics.
