Barbara Zipser (ed.), Medical Books in the Byzantine World (Bologna 2013) Barbara Zipser (ed.), Medical Books in the Byzantine World (Bologna 2013) This volume originates from a conference on Byzantine Medical Manuals in Context, held in central London on the 19th of September 2009. The conference, which was jointly organized by Peregrine Horden and Barbara Zipser, formed part of a three year project on Byzantine Medical Manuals: Construction and Use based at the Department of History at Royal Holloway University of London.
