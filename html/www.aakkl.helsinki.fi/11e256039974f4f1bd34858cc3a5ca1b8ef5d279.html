Melammu Digital Library

description
[First posted in AWOL 23 March 2012. Updated 12 September 2014]. The Melammu Project: The Intellectual Heritage of Assyria and Babylonia in East and West. The Melammu Project investigates the continuity, transformation and diffusion of Mesopotamian and Ancient Near Eastern culture from the third millennium BCE through the ancient world until Islamic times. It has two main activities: to organize conferences, and to provide resources relevant to the project on its website.
