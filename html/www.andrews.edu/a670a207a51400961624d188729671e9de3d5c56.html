Newsletter: The Institute of Archaeology, Siegfried H. Horn Archaeological Museum. The Institute of Archaeology, through the Horn Archaeological Museum, publishes a quarterly newsletter. Museum members and other interested individuals find a broad spectrum of valuable notes and announcements: activities of personnel, research reports, schedules of future events and projects, and notices of publications, as well as a variety of short notes. The Newsletter is published in hardcopy and electronic formats.
