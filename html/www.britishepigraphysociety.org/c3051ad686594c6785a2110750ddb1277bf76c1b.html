The British Epigraphy Society: BES News. Since its foundation, the Society has disseminated news pertaining to epigraphic events, discoveries and training opportunities to members on a regular basis, originally through the medium of a (bi)annual Newsletter. Today, BES news is disseminated in a variety of forms, including fortnighly e-mail shots and announcements on this website, whilst a NewsDigest functions primarily as an archive for many of the Society's activities. Here we provide links to a sample of Newsletters from 2003 to 2010, as well as a link to the most recent NewsDigest.
