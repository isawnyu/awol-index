Eachtra Journal. ISSN 2009-2237. It is our intention to use this web-based Journal to "narrate our work". As we complete research articles, lectures, posters and other documents we will publish them here. Miscellaneous items of interest will also be recorded along with upcoming events. In this way we intend to marry the strong Irish tradition of publication in journals with modern web-based media. Browse our Journal by Issues or use our Indexes: Articles - Miscellany - Events.
