Dig-it-al NEA

description
Posted today in This Forum stems from the session "Teaching Archaeology to Undergraduates and K-12 Schoolchildren" co-chaired by Ellen D. Bedell and Eric H. Cline at the 2010 ASOR Annual Meeting in Atlanta. The theme of the 2010 session was the use of technology to teach archaeology. Four of the presentations are being made available on Dig-it-al NEA in hopes that these papers inspire others to incorporate similar techniques and technology into their own curriculum.
