The Bible in Its Traditions

description
The Bible in Its Traditions is a project of the École Biblique et Archéologique Française de Jérusalem, the creators of the Jerusalem Bible. The goal of this project We intend to create the most extensive and helpful set of notes for the entire bible, with information of interest both to biblical scholars and casual readers.
