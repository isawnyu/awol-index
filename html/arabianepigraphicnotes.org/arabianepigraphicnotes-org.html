Arabian Epigraphic Notes: An Open Access Online Journal on Arabian EpigraphyISSN: 2451-8875 Arabian Epigraphic Notes (ISSN: 2451-8875) is a journal of the Leiden Center for the Study of Ancient Arabia. It is dedicated to the publication of epigraphy from Arabia, and for the discussion of relevant historical and linguistic issues.
