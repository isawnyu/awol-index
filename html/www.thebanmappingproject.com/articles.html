Theban Mapping Project Index of Articles. As a companion to the Atlas of the Valley of the Kings, this compilation of articles provides a broader understanding of the history and development of the Valley of the Kings and the cultural practices that have influenced and shaped the construction and utilization of the tombs in it. Copious photographs, maps and drawings illustrate each of these articles, authored by the Theban Mapping Project.
