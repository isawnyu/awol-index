Coptic Bibliographies The bibliographies listed below are the result of teaching Coptic history and language here at Macquarie. Their aim is to introduce students to an area of study. First Millennium Bibliography. An overview of Egyptian history and culture in the First Millennium CE. Coptic Language Bibliography. A bibliography of the Coptic language with a focus on the Sahidic dialect. Coptic Dialects Bibliography. A bibliography of the other regional forms of the Coptic language excluding Sahidic Coptic. Coptic Art and Archaeology Bibliography. A bibliography of Coptic Art and Archaeology Coptic Funerary Stelae Bibliography. A bibliography of Funerary Stelae written in Coptic.
